ProZone Sports Academy was founded in the year 2018 aiming to set higher
bars in field of Sportsand Fitness in India.

PSA provides multisport services such as Structured Physical Education Programmes, Sports
Management Services, Conducting Professional Coaching Programmes,
Personal Training Programmes providing plethora of indoor and outdoor
gaming opportunity to Increasing Sports Stars of India.

Under the professional guidance of expert coaches, PSA not only works toward best
on-field training but also aims to enlighten the youth by conducting special Health,
Fitness and physical Education classes.
